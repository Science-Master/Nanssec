MISSION STATEMENT

Nandom Senior High School seeks to promote a holistic development of the youth, for Christian civic responsibility and national development through continuous quest for academic excellence, integrity and service.

VISION STATEMENT

Inspired by Christian values, Nandom Senior High School is dedicateed to providing the youth with quality educaation, self-discipline and sound Christian moral education for achieving holistic human development and for nation building.

CORE VALUES

They include: Idealism, generosity, professionalism, integrity, diligence, innovativeness, mutual love and accountability.
